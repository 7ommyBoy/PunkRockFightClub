PunkRock FightClub was established to help young punk rockers to stay away froma addiction. To learn to have self control and use that energery into being productive.

It All Started With An Idea! As young punks we were introduce to intoxicate ourself at shows and just go all out rebel. This Community that "TomTom" and "Saus" started was for those punks to stay away from bad habits. To use that aggression into fighting and staying fit. To stay sober and live a productive life, and still be a bad ass at the same time.

Giving back to the community is one of our main prority. We have toy drives, charity runs, and live events. We donate all to the community because we are not a burden to our community. We are here to help those in need.
